About

CoSH is a website that helps students to convert their score intro letter, find a square root of a number, and find a hypotenuse of the triangle. The name 'CoSH' itself is an abbreviation from Convert, Square root, and Hypotenuse. CoSH will help many students to solve simple math problems. This website's purpose is to fulfill my assignment in Psychology and Design Thinking Subject. Hopefully, this website can be helpful for someone who needs it.
